## Brief Method
- — We present a novel autonomous robot navigation algorithm for outdoor environments that is capable of handling diverse terrain traversability conditions.
- We validate our method on a legged robot (Ghost Vision 60) and a wheeled robot (Clearpath Husky), in diverse real-world outdoor environments with different deformable and slippery terrains.
- Our approach, VLMGroNav, uses vision-language models (VLMs) and integrates them with physical grounding that is used to assess intrinsic terrain properties such as deformability and slipperiness.
