## Brief Method
- We adopt a 1:7 multimodal-to-action training ratio, twice the ratio in ECoT and ChatVLA (1:3), reducing the additional computation needed to maintain multimodal ability.
- Owing to our training paradigm, multimodal capabilities are preserved easily.
- The action expert employs a 12-layer transformer backbone with a hidden size of 768.
